## Part 7: Risk Mitigation

### What Could Go Wrong

**Risk 1: Model Inaccuracy**
- **Mitigation:** Continuous validation with real races
- **Mitigation:** Human-in-the-loop (crew chief final decision)
- **Mitigation:** Confidence intervals show uncertainty

**Risk 2: Team Adoption**
- **Mitigation:** Gradual rollout
- **Mitigation:** Training and support
- **Mitigation:** Start with non-critical decisions

**Risk 3: Overfitting**
- **Mitigation:** Track-specific, not race-specific
- **Mitigation:** Ensemble methods
- **Mitigation:** Regular model retraining

**Risk 4: Data Quality**
- **Mitigation:** Data validation checks
- **Mitigation:** Handle missing data gracefully
- **Mitigation:** Multiple data sources
